Nua Hair Colour

Our unique formula is infused with organic seaweed, meticulously handpicked along the pristine shores of Donegal, Ireland.

This not only ensures an unparalleled quality and purity but also harnesses the natural nourishing properties of the ocean. Nua Colour's commitment to health and sustainability is evident in our selection of natural ingredients, including aloe vera, argan oil, chamomile, and avocado oil, which work in harmony to leave hair silky, shiny, and visibly healthy.
