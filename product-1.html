  • Description

    Our permanent hair colour is enriched in certified organic ingredients, Seaweed and Argan Oil, Coconut oil with Aloe Vera. Natural vibrant colours with long lasting finish, full coverage shine and condition.

  • How to

    For professional use only. For Grey coverage - Mixing ratio 1:1. For Toning/Demi permanent - Mixing ratio 1:2.

  • Ingredients

    AQUA, STEARYL ALCOHOL, CETYL ALCOHOL, ETHANOLAMINE, GLYCOL DISTEARATE, CETEARETH-20, FRAGRANCE, PROPYLENE GLYCOL, OLEIC ACID, LAURETH-4, ARGANIA SPINOSA KERNEL OIL, COCOS NUCIFERA OIL, SPIRULINA SEAWEED, ALOE BARBADENSIS LEAF JUICE, SODIUM METABISULFITE, SODIUM ASCORBATE, TETRASODIUM EDTA, P-TOLUENEDIAMINE SULFATE, M-AMINOPHENOL, RESORCINOL, 4-AMINO-2-HYDROXYTOLUENE, 2-AMINO-4-HYDROXYETHYLAMINOANISOLE SULFATE.

  • Warnings

    Follow instructions. Avoid contact with eyes. Use appropriate gloves. Not for children. In case of irritation rinse and seek medical advice.
